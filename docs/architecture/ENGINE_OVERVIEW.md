# Engine Overview

- AI Director: coordinates stages and blocks invalid production.
- Story Engine: converts approved material into narrative outlines.
- Episode Engine: organizes episode progression.
- Scene Engine: converts scripts into scenes.
- Shot Engine: converts scenes into single-action shots.
- Prompt Compiler: composes engine-ready prompts.
- Continuity Engine: tracks identity, timeline, wardrobe, locations, props, and emotion.
- Research Engine: organizes questions and sources.
- Source Validation Engine: checks source authority and approval.
- Asset Manager: resolves shared and project-local assets.
- QA Engine: validates production readiness.
- Export Engine: packages deliverables.
- Engine Adapters: isolate provider-specific formatting for Flow, Gemini, Veo, Kling,
  Runway, Hailuo, Seedance, and future engines.

# Storyteller Performance Notes

Status: pending source validation before final recording

- Voice: Urdu documentary storyteller, mature and composed.
- Emotion: serious, reflective, morally gripping.
- Delivery: slow enough for a 3-5 minute episode, with meaningful pauses.
- Do not perform Allah's voice.
- Say sacred narration through storyteller framing, for example: `Allah Ta'ala ne farmaya...`
- Use `Qur'an ka mafhoom hai...` where exact verified translation is not yet locked.
- Do not perform Iblis as a character voice.
- Do not dramatize angels or Adam عليه السلام through dialogue.
- Avoid horror, rage, theatrical villain tone, or fantasy-battle energy.
- Final line should land quietly, not loudly.

# Depiction Policy

The production system distinguishes between:

1. Normal historical characters
2. Unnamed background populations
3. Revered Islamic personalities
4. Prophets
5. Sacred or highly sensitive events

For Prophets and revered Islamic personalities:

- Do not create direct facial depictions.
- Do not create reusable face identities.
- Do not create character sheets showing their faces.
- Use non-figurative storytelling approaches where appropriate.

Allowed production approaches may include:

- environment-based storytelling
- reactions of surrounding people
- objects and locations
- shadows that do not define identity
- distant composition
- doors, tents, landscapes, paths, architecture
- calligraphy or title cards
- symbolic transitions
- narrator-led explanation
- presence metadata

The presence library must contain metadata and production guidance, not facial visual
identity prompts.

# Authenticity Policy

Insan is a research-first project. Religious and historical claims must be traced to
source records before they are used in narration, scripts, visual context, prompts,
or QA-approved outputs.

No source is considered verified until reviewed and marked with an approved state in
`01_research/source_registry.yaml`.

Weak, disputed, or uncertain material must be labeled and must not be presented as
certainty.

# Source Hierarchy

Priority order:

1. Qur'an
2. Authentic Hadith
3. Reliable Tafsir
4. Established Seerah sources
5. Carefully evaluated classical histories
6. Scholarly interpretation
7. Modern academic references for geography, archaeology, chronology, and material culture

Historical details must not contradict Qur'an or authentic Hadith.

Weak or disputed reports must be labeled. Disputed content must not be presented as
certainty.

Dramatic storytelling must never fabricate religious claims.

Invented dialogue must not be attributed to sacred or historical personalities as
authentic speech.

Where exact details are unknown, narration must acknowledge uncertainty.

# Background Score Direction

Status: pending production approval

The score should be solemn, ancient, restrained, and emotionally powerful.

Allowed:

- Low warm drones
- Deep bowed textures
- Sparse frame drum pulse if extremely subtle
- Soft breath-like ambience
- Minimal low strings or oud-like textures without lyrical melody
- Long silence around sacred moments

Avoid:

- Horror music
- Fantasy battle music
- Excessive choir
- Inappropriate sacred vocalization
- Loud percussion
- Triumphant superhero tone
- Melodrama

The score must support reflection, not spectacle.

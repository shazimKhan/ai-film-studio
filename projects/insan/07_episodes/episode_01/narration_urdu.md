## 01 - Storyteller Opening

Yeh kahani hai insan ki. Us insan ki jo kabhi apni taqat par itna fakhr karta
hai ke kamzor ki aah sunai dena band ho jati hai. Jo zameen par akad kar
chalta hai, jaise us ke qadam kabhi rukenge hi nahi. Magar takabbur ki yeh
kahani insan se shuru nahi hui. Is ki jad us lamhe tak jati hai jab abhi
zameen par insan ki zindagi shuru bhi nahi hui thi.

## 02 - Before Humanity

Qur'an humein batata hai ke insan ke aane se pehle bhi Allah ki makhlooq
maujood thi. Jinn bhi the. Farishte bhi the. Farishte apne Rabb ke hukm ke
farmabardar the. Aur Iblis, Qur'an ke bayan ke mutabiq, jinn mein se tha.

## 03 - The Divine Announcement

Qur'an ka mafhoom hai ke Allah Ta'ala ne farishton se farmaya ke woh zameen
mein ek khalifah banane wala hai. Yeh aik azeem elan tha. Is elan mein insan
ki ibtida bhi thi, zimmedari bhi thi, aur imtihan ka raasta bhi.

## 04 - Adam's Creation

Qur'an aur Hadith ke zariye humein maloom hota hai ke Adam عليه السلام ki
takhleeq mitti aur gandhi hui mitti ke bayan se juri hai. Yeh tafseel hamare
liye itni hi hai jitni wahy aur sahih riwayat se sabit ho. Jo cheez wazeh nahi,
us mein hum apni taraf se manzar nahi banate.

## 05 - The Command

Phir woh lamha aaya jis ka zikr Qur'an kai maqamat par karta hai. Allah Ta'ala
ne farishton ko hukm diya ke Adam عليه السلام ko sajdah karein. Yeh sajdah
Allah ke hukm ki ita'at tha. Is ko Adam عليه السلام ki ibadat ke taur par
pesh nahi kiya jayega.

## 06 - Obedience And Refusal

Farishton ne hukm maana. Unhon ne sajdah kiya. Lekin Iblis ne inkar kar diya.
Yahan se takabbur ka pehla bada imtihan samne aata hai: hukm ke muqable mein
apni soch ko bara samajhna.

## 07 - The Claim Of Superiority

Qur'an ka mafhoom hai ke Iblis ne kaha ke woh Adam se behtar hai: use aag se
paida kiya gaya, aur Adam ko mitti se. Yeh sirf ek jawab nahi tha. Yeh apni
asal ko doosre ki asal se behtar samajhne ka elaan tha.

## 08 - The Fall Of Arrogance

Takabbur insan ko sirf doosron se door nahi karta. Takabbur banda ko haq ke
samne jhukne se rok deta hai. Iblis ka masla ilm ki kami nahi tha. Masla yeh
tha ke us ne hukm ko pehchan kar bhi apne ghamand ko chuna.

## 09 - Expulsion

Qur'an ke bayan ke mutabiq Iblis ko nikala gaya, zaleel kiya gaya, aur us par
lanat ka zikr aata hai. Yeh anjam sirf nafarmani ka nahi, balkeh takabbur bhari
nafarmani ka tha.

## 10 - Respite

Iblis ne mohlat maangi. Qur'an ke mafhoom ke mutabiq use ek muqarrar waqt tak
mohlat di gayi. Yeh mohlat izzat nahi thi. Yeh ek imtihan ke silsile ka hissa
thi.

## 11 - Declared Enmity

Phir Iblis ne apna irada zahir kiya: woh insan ko gumrah karne ki koshish
karega. Qur'an is dushmani ko chhupa kar nahi batata. Iblis ka nishana insan
hai, us ka iman hai, us ki shukr guzari hai, aur us ka raasta hai.

## 12 - The Protected Servants

Lekin Qur'an yeh bhi batata hai ke Allah ke mukhlis bandon par Iblis ka
ikhtiyar nahi. Gumrahi ka darwaza khulta hai, magar hidayat ka darwaza band
nahi hota.

## 13 - Closing Moral

Aaj jab insan apni zaat, apni quwwat, apni nasl, apni daulat ya apni soch par
ghuroor karta hai, to woh sirf ek buri aadat nahi apnata. Woh usi purane raste
ki taraf qadam badhata hai jahan hukm ke samne takabbur khara ho gaya tha.
Insan ki pehli hifazat yahi hai: haq ke samne jhuk jana, aur takabbur ko dil
mein ghar na banane dena.

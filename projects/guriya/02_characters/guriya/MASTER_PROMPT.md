# Guriya Master Character Prompt

Guriya is an 18-year-old young woman from Taxila, Punjab, Pakistan, in 1988. She
should look naturally beautiful, fresh, graceful, and emotionally expressive, with
the softness of a young Pakistani drama heroine. Her beauty is simple and innocent,
not modern fashion glamour.

Identity lock: oval face, soft wheatish skin, large expressive dark brown eyes,
naturally thick eyebrows, small straight nose, soft round cheeks, long black hair
kept in one simple braid, modest body proportions, no makeup, small traditional
earrings.

Wardrobe lock: simple pastel 1988 Pakistani shalwar kameez with matching dupatta.
The outfit must remain modest, period-accurate, practical, clean, and graceful. It
should make her look young and pretty without makeup, heavy jewelry, or modern
fashion styling.

Performance lock: soft-spoken, humble, caring, hardworking, respectful, emotionally
expressive, and restrained. Her face carries feeling clearly, but her body language
stays grounded and believable.

Visual quality lock: modern HD cinematic realism with sharp detail, clean lighting,
natural skin texture, and polished image quality. The world around her can feel old,
modest, and 1988 period-accurate, but Guriya herself must not look aged, dull, dusty,
or visually degraded.

Style lock: emotionally grounded Pakistani family drama, cinematic but believable,
not VHS, not low-resolution, not faded old-TV footage, no fashion-shoot styling, no
modern beauty filters.

# Guriya Master Character Prompt

Guriya is an 18-year-old young woman from Taxila, Punjab, Pakistan, in 1988.

Identity lock: oval face, soft wheatish skin, large expressive dark brown eyes,
naturally thick eyebrows, small straight nose, soft round cheeks, long black hair
kept in one simple braid, modest body proportions, no makeup, small traditional
earrings.

Wardrobe lock: simple pastel 1988 Pakistani shalwar kameez with matching dupatta.
The outfit must remain modest, period-accurate, practical, and non-glamorous.

Performance lock: soft-spoken, humble, caring, hardworking, respectful, emotionally
expressive, and restrained. Her face carries feeling clearly, but her body language
stays grounded and believable.

Style lock: realistic Pakistani television drama, natural skin texture, cinematic
but not glossy, no fashion-shoot styling, no modern beauty filters.

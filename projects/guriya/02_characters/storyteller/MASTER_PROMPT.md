# Storyteller Master Character Prompt

The storyteller is a recurring adult male narrator seated in a podcast-style studio.
He speaks directly to the audience with seriousness, compassion, and reflective
control.

He should feel like a grounded narrator for a Pakistani television drama, not a
comedian, influencer, or news anchor. His appearance must be finalized through
approved character references before production use.

Performance lock: calm eye contact, measured delivery, restrained hand movement,
compassionate seriousness, no theatrical exaggeration.

# Guriya Family Home Master Location Prompt

Modest family home in Taxila, Punjab, Pakistan, in 1988. The home feels lived-in,
practical, and emotionally warm: simple rooms, household sewing materials, modest
bedding, traditional domestic objects, natural textures, and period-accurate
details.

The environment should support a realistic Pakistani television drama tone. Nothing
should feel wealthy, staged, glossy, or modernized.

# Guriya Family Home Negative Prompt

- No modern appliances, smartphones, LED strips, contemporary plastic furniture,
  modern wall art, visible brand logos, urban luxury decor, or glossy interiors.
- No fantasy lighting, showroom styling, artificial glamour, subtitles, watermarks,
  text overlays, or UI elements.
- Do not make the home look newly built, wealthy, or outside 1988 Taxila.

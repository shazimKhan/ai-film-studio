# Episode 01 Story: Guriya

## Logline

An emotionally grounded introduction to Guriya, beginning with a storyteller frame
and moving into her late-night struggle before returning to her peaceful early life
in 1988 Taxila.

## Story Spine

1. The storyteller introduces Guriya with compassion and seriousness.
2. Guriya is shown sewing late at night while her children sleep nearby.
3. She pauses, looks at the children, and holds worry together with determination.
4. The episode moves into a flashback.
5. In 1988 Taxila, young Guriya lives with her parents and sisters.
6. Her kindness, innocence, hard work, sewing skill, and family bond are established.
7. The episode ends with a quiet hint that her future marriage will bring hardship.

## Production Intent

The episode should feel like a realistic Pakistani television drama: natural,
restrained, emotionally direct, period-accurate, and cinematic without glamour.

## Structural Notes

- Storyteller scenes alternate with Guriya’s dramatic scenes.
- Dialogue and narration live in `10_voiceover/`.
- Visual prompts should reference character and location prompt packs instead of
  repeating long descriptions.
- Motion prompts should describe movement, expression changes, environment motion,
  and camera movement only.
